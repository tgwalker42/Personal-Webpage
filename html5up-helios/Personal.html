Get to Know Me!

I am an avid outdoorsman! Being in the woods has always been my favorite place to be which is why working in conservation comes so natural for me. Some of my hobbies include hunting, fishing, archery, shooting, hiking, and much more. However every once in awhile I'll have an indoors day where I work on my artistic skills by painting, drawing, and woodburning some of the cool things I've seen while outside!

Fishing is what first made me become interested in wildlife and pursue opportunities in order to work with animals as a profession. Then once I started my college career I was surrounded by hunters as peers and soon caught on and became one myself! It only amplified my love for the outdoors and my drive to become a wildlife biologist.

My Painitng

Painting was a way for me to be able to still enjoy the outdoors when I was stuck inside due to weather, being sick, etc. But I honed my skills and now am a proud amateur painter of animals as you can tell in this red morph eastern screech owl.

Some of my other hobbies/skills

Radio Telemetry

I've tracked turtles, snakes, bats, and deer!

Herping

Salamanders are my second favorite wildlife animal and I love going out and searching for these guys!

Hiking

Getting out in the Blue Ridge and hiking to a ton of beautiful spots is fun! and even better when I can Herp or radio track along the way!
